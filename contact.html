Getting Here

Travel to Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord

The hotel offers convenient access to Düsseldorf city centre, Messe Düsseldorf, PSD Bank Dome and other key destinations by public transport.

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord is easy to reach by car and offers a spacious garage with 90 parking places for hotel guests.

Düsseldorf Airport is easily accessible from the hotel, making it ideal for business travellers, groups and transit guests.
