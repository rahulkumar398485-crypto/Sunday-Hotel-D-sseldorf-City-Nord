City Highlights

Experience Düsseldorf

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord is a convenient base for exploring Düsseldorf’s most popular attractions. From the city centre and Old Town to the Rhein Tower, the banks of the Rhine, Königsallee and Gehry Bauten, guests can enjoy culture, shopping, architecture and riverside views with easy access from the hotel.

Top Attractions

Discover the City

Düsseldorf City Centre

Explore Düsseldorf’s lively city centre with shopping, dining, business districts and cultural experiences.

Old Town

Discover Düsseldorf’s famous Old Town with traditional streets, restaurants, bars and historic charm.

Rhein Tower

Enjoy panoramic views over Düsseldorf and the Rhine from one of the city’s most recognisable landmarks.

Banks of the Rhine

Walk along the Rhine promenade and enjoy riverside views, cafés, architecture and a relaxed city atmosphere.

Königsallee

Visit Düsseldorf’s elegant shopping boulevard, known for luxury boutiques, cafés and its scenic canal.

Gehry Bauten

See the iconic Gehry buildings in Düsseldorf’s Media Harbour, a highlight for architecture lovers.
