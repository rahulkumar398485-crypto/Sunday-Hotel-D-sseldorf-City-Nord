Our Story

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord

A Modern Hotel for Business & Leisure

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord offers a welcoming and comfortable stay for both business and leisure travellers. Conveniently located with excellent transport connections, the hotel is ideal for work trips, city breaks, group stays and stopovers.

Comfort, Convenience and Hospitality

Guests can enjoy spacious accommodation, dining options and personalised hospitality in a relaxed atmosphere. The hotel features 190 comfortable non-smoking rooms, a restaurant, hotel bar, garden terrace and a spacious garage with 90 parking places.

Location

Düsseldorf City Nord

Located in Düsseldorf, the hotel offers perfect access to Messe Düsseldorf, Esprit Arena, Düsseldorf Airport, the city centre and PSD Bank Dome by car and public transport.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Düsseldorf City Centre
  • Old Town
  • Rhein Tower
  • Banks of the Rhine
  • Königsallee
  • Gehry Bauten

Why Guests Love Our Location

  • Excellent access to Messe Düsseldorf
  • Convenient for airport and transit guests
  • Ideal for business travellers and groups
  • Easy access by car and public transport
