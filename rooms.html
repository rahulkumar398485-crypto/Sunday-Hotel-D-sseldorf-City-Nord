Accommodation

Comfort & Convenience

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord offers 190 comfortable non-smoking rooms. All rooms are double rooms for single or double occupancy and include free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, a large workspace desk by the window and modern bathroom facilities with large shower, hairdryer, vanity mirror and heat lamp.

Pet-Friendly Room Options

Small dogs are welcome at Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord, making the hotel a convenient choice for guests travelling with pets.

A charge of €12.00 per dog per night applies. Please inform the hotel in advance when travelling with your dog so the team can prepare your stay.
