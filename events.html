Smart Event Facilities in Düsseldorf City Nord

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord offers six flexible facilities for small and large meetings, group stays, coach tours and corporate events. With capacity for meetings of up to 120 people, the hotel is a practical choice for business travellers and event organisers.

Tailored catering and accommodation solutions are available upon request, supported by comfortable rooms, restaurant facilities, hotel bar, garden terrace and convenient access to Messe Düsseldorf, the airport, city centre and PSD Bank Dome.
