A Smart Stay in Düsseldorf City Nord

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord offers a welcoming and comfortable stay for business and leisure travellers. With excellent transport connections, the hotel provides easy access to Messe Düsseldorf, the airport, city centre, PSD Bank Dome and major local attractions.
