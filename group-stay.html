Ideal for Group Travel in Düsseldorf

Sunday Hotel Düsseldorf City Nord is a convenient choice for groups, coach tours, business travellers and transit guests. With 190 comfortable non-smoking rooms, excellent transport connections, restaurant facilities, a hotel bar, garden terrace and spacious garage parking, the hotel offers everything needed for an easy and comfortable group stay in Düsseldorf.

Group Facilities

Everything Your Group Needs


190 Comfortable Rooms

Non-smoking double rooms for single or double occupancy with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TV, workspace desk and modern bathroom amenities.

Restaurant & Breakfast

A relaxed restaurant for international and regional cuisine, group dining experiences and a rich continental breakfast buffet served daily.

Meetings & Events

Six flexible facilities for small and large meetings, corporate events, group stays and coach tours with capacity for up to 120 people.

90 Parking Places

A spacious garage with 90 parking places makes arrival convenient for groups, business guests and travellers arriving by car.

Easy Access

Business & Transit

Perfect access to Messe Düsseldorf, Düsseldorf Airport, the city centre, PSD Bank Dome and Esprit Arena by car and public transport.

Tailored Group Solutions

Tailored catering and accommodation solutions are available upon request for coach tours, corporate stays and group travel.
